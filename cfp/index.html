üì® Submission

  • Submissions should describe unpublished original work.
  • Papers may consist of up to six (6) pages of content, including references and appendices.
  • All submitted papers must be written in English and conform to the double-column format of IEEE Signal Processing Society conferences.
  • The paper templates (word and latex) can be downloaded below:
  • O-COCOSDA 2025 (word)

Papers must be submitted through the EasyChair system by June 15, 2025 (see Important Dates below), via this link.
Accepted papers will be presented orally or as posters as determined by the committee. The conference proceedings will be submitted to the IEEE Xplore digital library. All the accepted papers must go through the file conversion offered by IEEE PDF eXpress. You can refer to the link here: https://ieee-pdf-express.org/account/login?ReturnUrl=%2F. The Conference ID is TBA.

O-COCOSDA 2025 is an on-site (in-person) conference. To ensure that all accepted papers are presented, the organizing committee will clearly communicate this requirement through multiple channels, including this website, the Call for Papers, the acceptance notification email, and during the registration process. Please note that papers that are not presented on site during the conference will not be included in the final proceedings nor submitted to IEEE Xplore.
