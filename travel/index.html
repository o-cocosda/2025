Travel

🛫 How to Get to Yogyakarta

  • By Air: Yogyakarta International Airport (YIA) is about 60–90 minutes from the city center. Direct international flights available from Kuala Lumpur (AirAsia, Batik Air), Singapore (Scoot, AirAsia), and Jakarta (multiple airlines).
  • By Train: Available from Jakarta and Surabaya to Tugu Station Yogyakarta.
  • By Road: Accessible via Trans Java Toll Road.

🧭 Transportation in Yogyakarta

  • Airport Rail Link from YIA to Tugu Station (city center) is available every 1–2 hours and takes ~40 minutes.
  • Grab & Gojek (ride-hailing apps widely available)
  • Official Airport Taxis
  • Car rentals with drivers
  • Trans Jogja Bus – cheap and convenient public transport

🌍 Visa Information

  • Visa-free entry is granted for ASEAN countries (e.g., Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam, etc.) for up to 30 days.
  • Other nationalities are required to obtain a Visa on Arrival (VOA) or apply for an e-Visa prior to travel.
  • Visa on Arrival (VOA) is available for over 90 countries including the 🇺🇸 USA, 🇬🇧 UK, 🇯🇵 Japan, 🇦🇺 Australia, 🇨🇳 China, 🇰🇷 South Korea, 🇫🇷 France, 🇩🇪 Germany, 🇳🇱 Netherlands, 🇮🇳 India, 🇹🇼 Taiwan, 🇨🇦 Canada, 🇳🇿 New Zealand, and more. VOA costs approximately Rp500,000 and is valid for 30 days (extendable once).
  • Visitors from non-ASEAN countries should consult the Indonesian Immigration website or their local embassy for the most updated visa requirements.
  • e-Visa application: evisa.imigrasi.go.id
  • Request invitation letter from the committee via email: ococosda2025@gmail.com

🏯 Tourist Attractions

  • Borobudur Temple (approx. 1.5 hours drive)
  • Yogyakarta Palace (Keraton)
  • Taman Sari (Water Castle)
  • Prambanan Temple
  • Malioboro Street

🍽️ Local Cuisine to Try

  • Gudeg – signature Yogyakarta dish
  • Sate Klathak
  • Bakpia Pathok – sweet mung bean pastry
  • Wedang Ronde or Es Dawet – traditional drinks

💡 Tips for International Visitors

  • Currency: Indonesian Rupiah (IDR)
  • Weather in November: warm with possible light rain – bring a light umbrella
  • Electricity: 220V, plug types C & F
  • Internet: Free Wi-Fi widely available in hotels and public areas
  • Useful apps: Grab, Gojek, Google Translate, Google Maps

🌤️ Weather Info

Yogyakarta in November usually has warm weather, with average temperatures around 28–32°C (82–90°F). Occasional light rain may occur—consider bringing light clothing and a small umbrella.
