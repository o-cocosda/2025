🧭 Directions

  • From Yogyakarta International Airport (YIA): Approximately 60–75 minutes by taxi or Grab.

  • From Malioboro Street: 5–10 minutes by car or 15 minutes walking.

  • 1O1 Style Yogyakarta Malioboro – A stylish modern hotel in a strategic area, approximately 1.5 km from UKDW.
